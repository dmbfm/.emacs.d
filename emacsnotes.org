** Packages

** Commands
*** Bookmakarks
- <C-x r m>: Set bookmark
- <C-x r b>: Jump to bookmark
- <C-x r l>: List bookmarks
*** Killing
- <M-SPC>: Kill white space, leaving only one between words.
- <M-\>: Delete horizontal space; kill white space, leaving no space between words.
